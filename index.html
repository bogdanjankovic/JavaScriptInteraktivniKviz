Hello, and welcome to QuizWizz !

A place where you can test your knowledge of random facts and challenge yourself to the fullest!

The rules are simple:
There are 9 rounds of 10 questions each, where each round represents a category .

Choose the correct answer out of 4 for each question, and if you choose wisely, you will be awarded with points based on the difficulty of the question.

If your choice is wrong, you lose, simple as that. But, worry not! For your quest to beat the highscore, you are accompanied with 2 supporting buttons that you can use each round!
